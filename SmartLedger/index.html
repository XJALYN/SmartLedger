SmartLedger

智能记账助手 - 首页是一个助手聊天对话框，支持小票上传 和 语音，文字输入， 用户输入语音文字或者图片后，推给一个用户消费明细，标题，金额，时间，分类，等信息，用户点击确认，保存一条账单信息，第二个tab是消费明细列表，支持名称和分类搜索，第三个tab是消费统计有各种维度的图标 第4个tab是设置页面，里面有很多设置，算力充值等等功能，主题色改变

Screens

  • AI Assistant Chat — User interacts with AI via voice, text, or receipt image to initiate expense logging.
  • Confirm Expense — User reviews and edits AI-extracted expense details before saving to ledger.
  • Expense Ledger — User browses, searches, and filters saved expense records by name or category.
  • Spending Analytics — User visualizes spending patterns across time, category, and custom dimensions.
  • Settings & Credits — User manages app preferences, theme colors, and AI compute credits.
